About NGO Connect

Empowering social change through technology and transparency

Our Mission

To create a transparent, efficient, and impactful ecosystem that connects NGOs, donors, and volunteers, enabling meaningful social change across India through technology-driven solutions.

Transparency

Complete visibility into how donations are utilized with real-time tracking and impact reports.

Community

Impact

Maximizing social impact through efficient resource allocation and verified outcomes.

Our Story

NGO Connect was born from a simple observation: while India has thousands of dedicated NGOs working tirelessly for social causes, there was a significant gap between these organizations and the people who wanted to support them.

Donors often struggled to find trustworthy organizations, volunteers couldn't easily discover meaningful opportunities, and NGOs faced challenges in reaching potential supporters. Most importantly, there was a lack of transparency in how donations were being utilized.

Founded in 2024, NGO Connect bridges these gaps by creating a unified platform that brings together all stakeholders in the social impact ecosystem. Our technology-driven approach ensures transparency, builds trust, and maximizes the impact of every contribution.
